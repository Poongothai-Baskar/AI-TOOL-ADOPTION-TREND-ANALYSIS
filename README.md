# AI Tool Adoption Trend Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes AI adoption trends across organizations using a dataset containing 200,000 records and 13 features. The analysis focuses on understanding AI adoption patterns, investment impact, employee training, AI maturity, productivity gains, and deployment trends.

The project applies data analysis, visualization, correlation analysis, hypothesis testing, and organization segmentation techniques to generate business insights and recommendations.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze AI adoption trends across industries.
* Study AI adoption growth over time.
* Evaluate the relationship between AI investment and productivity gain.
* Examine the impact of employee AI training on AI adoption.
* Analyze AI maturity and deployment patterns.
* Perform hypothesis testing on AI maturity and AI adoption.
* Segment organizations based on AI performance indicators.
* Generate business recommendations for successful AI implementation.

---

## Analysis Performed

* AI Maturity vs Deployment Count

### Correlation Analysis

* Correlation Matrix

### Hypothesis Testing

**Null Hypothesis (H₀):**
AI maturity has no significant effect on AI adoption levels.

**Alternative Hypothesis (H₁):**
AI maturity significantly affects AI adoption levels.

### Result

* P-value = 0.0000
* Decision = Reject H₀

Conclusion: AI maturity significantly affects AI adoption levels.
